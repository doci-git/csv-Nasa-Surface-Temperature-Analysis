GISS Surface Temperature Analysis (GISTEMP v4)

The GISS Surface Temperature Analysis ver. 4 (GISTEMP v4) is an estimate of global surface temperature change. Graphs and tables are updated around the middle of every month using current data files from NOAA GHCN v4 (meteorological stations) and ERSST v5 (ocean areas). These updated files incorporate reports for the previous month and also late reports and corrections for earlier months.
